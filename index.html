I believe a brand’s most valuable asset is a trusted voice. I specialize in building that authority through strategic Public Relations and high impact copywriting that ensures a brand’s presence remains influential and enduring.

Hi, I'm Mohamad Jibran — a Public Relations Enthusiast & Copywriter based in Jakarta, Indonesia. My work exists at the vital intersection of communication strategy and creative execution: that precise moment when the right words reach the right audience and spark genuine action.

With a background spanning Marketing, Brand strategy, and High impact content, I am deeply invested in how brands navigate modern culture, cultivate trust, and articulate stories that truly resonate. Beyond the strategic realm, my experience as a commercial model sharpens my instincts for aesthetics, presence, and the nuances of visual communication.

I also dedicate time to writing about music, film, culture, and other subjects that ignite my interest. This practice fuels a constant state of curiosity a quality I believe is the most valuable asset one can bring to any creative brief or business challenge.
